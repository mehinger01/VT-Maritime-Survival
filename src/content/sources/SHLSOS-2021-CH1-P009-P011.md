## What this file is (and isn't)

This is **not** a transcription of the manual's pages 9-11. Import Pack 001
(`src/content/imports/import-pack-001.json`) supplied a *summary* of this
page range — a list of concept titles and vocabulary terms — not the
manual's own text. Per `docs/CONTENT_INTAKE_BENCHMARK.md`'s own rule,
"traced to" a source means a specific page can be pointed to and read, not
that a summary is plausible. This file exists to hold that place in the
`/content/sources` layout the pipeline expects, honestly labeled as
incomplete rather than presented as verified text.

## Also flagging: edition mismatch

the manual already confirmed for Vinci's actual course is the **"revised
June 2024"** edition. These may be the same manual with an editions gap, or
they may not be — worth confirming with Vinci before treating this pack's
content as authoritative for his specific exam.
